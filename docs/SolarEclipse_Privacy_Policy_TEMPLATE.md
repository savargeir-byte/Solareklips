# Privacy Policy for Solar Eclipse

**App Name:** Solar Eclipse  
**Bundle ID:** com.solareklips.eclipseapp  
**Last Updated:** December 23, 2025  
**Effective Date:** December 23, 2025

## 1. Overview
Solar Eclipse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your device's GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- **Why we collect it:** To provide accurate eclipse timing and visibility information specific to your geographic location
- **How we use it:** Location data is processed locally on your device to calculate eclipse times. We do NOT store your location on our servers.
- **Permission required:** Yes - you can deny location access, but eclipse timing features will use a default location (Reykjavik, Iceland)

### 2.2 Device Motion Data
- **What we collect:** Accelerometer and gyroscope sensor data
- **Why we collect it:** To create interactive shadow effects during eclipse visualization (tilt feature)
- **How we use it:** Motion data is processed in real-time on your device and is NOT transmitted anywhere
- **Permission required:** Yes - you can deny motion sensor access, but tilt-based shadow effects will not work

### 2.3 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)
- **What AdMob collects:** Device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP address, device type, app interactions
- **Why AdMob collects it:** To display personalized advertisements and measure ad performance
- **How AdMob uses it:** Please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [AdMob's Data Usage](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
- **Your choices:** You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)

### 2.4 Map Tile Data
- **Service:** OpenStreetMap (OSM)
- **What's collected:** IP address when loading map tiles
- **Why:** To display eclipse path maps
- **Policy:** [OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

### 3.1 Local Storage Only
- All eclipse event data is stored locally on your device
- No user accounts or personal profiles are created
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

### 3.2 We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Name, email, or phone number
- ❌ Photos or camera access
- ❌ Contacts or calendar
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Purchase history
- ❌ User accounts or passwords

### 6.2 Your GDPR Rights
If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:
- ✅ Access your data (we don't store personal data on servers)

- ✅ Delete your data (uninstall the app)

## 7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

### 7.1 California Residents
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

### 7.2 "Do Not Sell My Info"
