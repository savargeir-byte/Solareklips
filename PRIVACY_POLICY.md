## Quick Summary

### What We Collect

- **GPS Location:** For accurate eclipse timing at your location
- **Motion Sensors:** For interactive shadow effects
- **Ad Data:** Google AdMob collects device info for ads

### What We Don't Collect

- No user accounts or personal information
- No data stored on our servers
- All eclipse data stored locally on your device

### Your Rights

- Deny location/motion permissions anytime
- All data processing happens on your device
- No data sharing except with AdMob for ads
